About This Demo

What is being tracked?

This demonstration website is designed to showcase a comprehensive visitor tracking system. Every interaction you make is being monitored and recorded, including:

  • Page Views: Every page you visit is recorded with timestamp and referrer information
  • Click Events: Clicks on buttons, links, and other interactive elements
  • Scroll Behavior: How far you scroll down the page
  • Session Information: Your unique visitor ID and session duration
  • Custom Events: Specific actions that can be tracked programmatically

Visitor Classification

The system automatically classifies visitors into three categories:

New Visitors

First-time visitors to the website

Returning Visitors

Visitors who have been here before

Bots

Automated traffic and crawlers

Real-time Analytics

All tracking data is processed in real-time and displayed in the analytics dashboard. You can see your activity appear in the live visitor feed within seconds of your interactions.

The system provides comprehensive insights including visitor flows, popular pages, referrer information, and detailed event tracking that helps understand user behavior patterns.

Privacy and Data Protection

This tracking system is designed with privacy in mind. IP addresses are hashed for anonymity, and no personally identifiable information is stored. The system respects Do Not Track settings and provides options for users to opt out of tracking.
